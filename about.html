About GIET Polytechnic College

GIET Polytechnic College is a premier institution under GIET Campus, located in Rajahmundry, Andhra Pradesh. Known for quality technical education, it fosters innovation, practical skills, and discipline.

Our Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in technical education by fostering innovation, leadership, and holistic development.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality technical education and training.
  • To prepare industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values.
  • To encourage entrepreneurship, research, and lifelong learning.

Why Choose GIET Polytechnic?

  • Experienced and dedicated faculty.
  • Modern labs and infrastructure.
  • Placement assistance and industry tie-ups.
  • Vibrant campus life and student clubs.

Departments

We offer diploma programs in CME, AI & MI, CNN, Mining, and Automobile Engineering.

Principal's Message

Welcome to GIET Polytechnic College. We believe in the power of education to transform lives. Our mission is to shape young minds into skilled professionals and responsible citizens. We strive to create a dynamic learning environment that fosters innovation, integrity, and excellence.

- Principal, GIET Polytechnic College
