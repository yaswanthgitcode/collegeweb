Diploma Programs at GIET Polytechnic College

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering diploma program prepares students to design, develop, and maintain mechanical systems. The curriculum covers thermodynamics, materials science, manufacturing, and CAD/CAM.

Civil Engineering

This program focuses on construction, infrastructure design, and project management. Students learn structural engineering, surveying, and environmental engineering techniques.

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

The Electrical & Electronics diploma covers power systems, circuits, control systems, and instrumentation, preparing students for careers in energy and electronics sectors.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

This course emphasizes communication systems, microprocessors, signal processing, and embedded systems with hands-on training.

Computer Engineering (CME)

The Computer Engineering diploma includes programming, data structures, networking, databases, and software engineering fundamentals to prepare students for IT industries.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Intelligence (AI & MI)

This emerging program introduces students to AI concepts, machine learning algorithms, robotics, and data analytics to meet the growing demand in AI fields.

Computer Network & Networking (CNN)

Students learn about network architecture, protocols, security, and administration to become network professionals in the telecom and IT sectors.

Mining Engineering

This program covers mining techniques, mineral processing, safety, and environmental aspects, preparing students for careers in mining industries.

Automobile Engineering

The Automobile Engineering diploma focuses on vehicle design, manufacturing, diagnostics, and maintenance, equipping students for the automotive industry.
